2. First things HR should do — Add a Job Posting 1. Go to Dashboard: http://127.0.0.1:8000/
   (If you built dashboard as home route.) 2. Click Add Job (or visit http://127.0.0.1:8000/job/add/). 3. Fill the form:
   • Title: e.g. Python Developer
   • Description: job summary/responsibilities
   • Required Skills: comma-separated, e.g. Python, Django, REST, PostgreSQL
   • Min Experience: integer (years), e.g. 2 4. Click Save Job. The job now appears on the dashboard.

Why: this job record is used to evaluate/score each uploaded resume.
